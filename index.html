What is Shark Tank?

Innovation thrives when ideas meet opportunity. Shark Tank is a premier platform where aspiring entrepreneurs and college students showcase their groundbreaking business ideas and project innovations. This competition invites participants—either solo or in teams—to pitch their ideas before an expert panel comprising industry leaders, investors, and mentors.

Participants will present their innovative solutions, receive constructive feedback, and get the chance to refine their concepts based on expert insights. The event encourages creativity, problem-solving, and entrepreneurial thinking, helping students transform their ideas into real-world ventures.

What to expect from this competition?

A college-level Shark Tank competition is a dynamic platform that encourages innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurial thinking. It provides students with a real-world experience that challenges them to develop and present their business ideas in a competitive environment.

Modes of competition

Participants from other states can join through our online mode, ensuring distance is no barrier to innovation. Meanwhile, those from the same state can experience the competition in person through our offline mode, allowing for face-to-face interactions, networking, and live pitching.
